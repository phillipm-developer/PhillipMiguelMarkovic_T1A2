AI Generated Bonsai

March 02 2023 / Bonsai Empire

A trend that was long in the making is quickly becoming mainstream - AI generated texts and images. We tried generating Bonsai designs and were amazed with some of the outcomes!

The era of artificial intelligence and its application to create texts and images is upon us. You have probably heard of ChatGPT, which uses AI to create impressive and realistic texts? Well add Midjourney to the mix to create stunning images and artwork.

The good news - it is free to use and provides impressive designs (see below). The bad news; although each outcome is unique, often it uses styles that are most common on the internet, plus it remains to be seen who owns the rights to AI designs based on other artists known works!

How AI can be used for us in the Bonsai community remains to be seen. What follows below is an overview of some Bonsai artworks we created, all using only AI!
